    Asus VivoBook S15 S513EA Core i5 11th Gen FHD OLED Laptop

    Asus VivoBook S15 S513EA, available at TechLand BD, is a sleek and powerful laptop that offers a range of impressive features. Powered by an 11th Gen Intel Core i5 1135G7 processor with a base frequency of 2.40 GHz and a max turbo frequency of 4.20 GHz, this laptop ensures smooth and efficient performance. It comes with 8GB DDR4 RAM and a 512GB NVMe PCIe SSD, providing ample space for all your files and applications. The laptop features a 15.6-inch FHD OLED display with a resolution of 1920x1080, offering vibrant and sharp visuals.
